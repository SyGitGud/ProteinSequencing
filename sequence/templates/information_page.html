About the Product

The product is developed by the members of Team 5 for COMP 496: Senior Project II. This product uses an AI, transformer model for protein scaffolding.

Vision

The product's vision is to create an informative introduction to working with models that predict complete protein scaffolds from incomplete protein scaffolds by using a clean and detailed interface.

What does the model do?

The model contextualizes patterns in training datasets.

How does the model work?

The model uses the k-nearest neighbor (KNN) algorithm to take an incomplete scaffold, fill in the scaffold gaps, and output the complete scaffold and training data.

What is the purpose of the model?

The purpose of the model is to predict and complete protein sequences that are only partially known to researchers.

How to use this product?

  1. Select a scaffold from the dropdown menu.
  2. Enter the incomplete scaffold in the textbox.
  3. Press the enter key.
  4. The complete scaffold, output accuracy, and training sequence will be displayed to the corresponding textfield.

What are protein scaffolds?

Protein scaffolds are molecules of protein that binds to other protein.

Which database is used for the product?

The database used for the product is MySQL.
