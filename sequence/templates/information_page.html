About the Product

The product is developed by the members of Team 5 for COMP 496: Senior Project II. This product uses an AI, KNN model for protein scaffolding.

What the model does, and how does it work?

The model takes an incomplete scaffold, fills in the scaffold gaps, and outputs the complete scaffold and training data.

How to use this product?

  1. Select a scaffold from the dropdown menu.
  2. Input the incomplete scaffold in the textbox.
  3. Press enter

What are protein scaffolds?

Protein scaffolds are molecules of protein that binds to other protein.

Which database is used for the product?

The database used for the product is MySQL.
